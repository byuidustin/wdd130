Welcome to the Sky3Dprints Home page. This websites goal is to provide links to websites that will allow you to order models created by 3d modelers like Cinderwing and Heroic Studios. All which will be shown on these pages. This website serves as a way for people to buy custom 3d printed models that are articulated as well as cosplay props from me or to provide links to the creators of these products so that if you have a 3D printer you to can make some of these awesome products for yourself.

Hi, my name is Dustin Webster I am currently 28 living with my wife and 4 kids. My interests are 3d printing and 3D model building I hope to open a small shop that specializes in making and painting models for cosplayers and tabletop board game enthusiast. I currently am working on my painting skills in order to paint large scale miniture sets like those in Warhammer 40K.
